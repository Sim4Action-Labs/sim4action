## Feedback Loop Catalog

### Ecological Feedback Loops

#### Loop 1: Stock Biomass Management
- Path: Stock Biomass → Fishing Activity → Bycatch Levels → Stock Biomass
- Type: Balancing
- Strength: Strong
- Evidence: Stock assessment data, catch records
- Temporal Scale: Medium-term (1-3 years)
- Spatial Scale: Regional

#### Loop 2: Environmental Productivity
- Path: Water Temperature → Ocean Productivity → Stock Recruitment → Stock Biomass → Water Temperature
- Type: Reinforcing
- Strength: Moderate
- Evidence: Environmental monitoring, research studies
- Temporal Scale: Long-term (5+ years)
- Spatial Scale: Regional

### Economic Feedback Loops

#### Loop 3: Revenue-Cost Cycle
- Path: Fishery Revenue → Operating Costs → Fishery Revenue
- Type: Balancing
- Strength: Strong
- Evidence: Financial records, industry reports
- Temporal Scale: Short-term (seasonal)
- Spatial Scale: Local

#### Loop 4: Market Integration
- Path: Product Price → Export Volume → Fishery Revenue → Product Price
- Type: Reinforcing
- Strength: Moderate
- Evidence: Trade statistics, market reports
- Temporal Scale: Medium-term (1-3 years)
- Spatial Scale: Global

- Strength: Strong
- Evidence: Census data, social surveys
- Temporal Scale: Medium-term (1-3 years)

#### Loop 6: Indigenous Rights Integration
