## Query Introduction

I am conducting research for the Blue Economy CRC's Seafood Futures project in Australia. We are developing causal system models for five key Australian fisheries/aquaculture systems. I need comprehensive information about the Northern Prawn Fisheries (NPF) to identify key variables, relationships, and dynamics for causal mapping. The information will be presented as a standardized "Fishery Systems Profile" that follows a specific format for cross-comparison with other fishery systems.

## Research Focus

Please conduct thorough research on the Northern Prawn Fisheries (NPF) in Australia, focusing specifically on identifying system components and relationships that would be relevant for constructing a causal map.
