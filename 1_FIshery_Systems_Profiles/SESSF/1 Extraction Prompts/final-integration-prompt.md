## Role and Approach
As the integration analyst, you will:
1. Synthesize findings from the five phase documents (SESSF_Phase_1.md through SESSF_Phase_5.md)
2. Identify cross-phase connections and themes

Your role is not to conduct new research but to organize and synthesize the existing analyses into a comprehensive, coherent document that captures the full system profile.
