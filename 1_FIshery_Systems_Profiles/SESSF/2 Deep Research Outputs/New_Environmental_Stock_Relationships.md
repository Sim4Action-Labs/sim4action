## Proposed New Relationships

### 1. Upwelling Intensity → Species Distribution
```
From Variable: V49 (Upwelling Intensity)
To Variable: V12 (Species Distribution)
Type: Variable
Strength: Medium
Primary Justification: Upwelling brings nutrient-rich water affecting species distribution patterns
Supporting Evidence:
- Empirical: IMOS data showing species aggregations in upwelling areas
- Theoretical: Well-established ecological principle of species following productivity
- System Context: Links physical processes to biological distribution
Confidence: High
Time Scale: Short to Medium-term
Spatial Scale: Regional
```
