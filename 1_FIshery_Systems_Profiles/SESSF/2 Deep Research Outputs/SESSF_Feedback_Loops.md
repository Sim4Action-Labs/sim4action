# Southern and Eastern Scalefish and Shark Fishery: Feedback Loops Analysis

This document contains the extracted feedback loops from the SESSF Phase 3 System Dynamics Analysis.

## Feedback Loops

| Loop ID | Loop Name | Components | Type | Evidence | Strength | System Impact |
|---------|-----------|------------|------|----------|----------|---------------|
| L1 | Stock-recruitment feedback | Stock depletion -> reduced recruitment -> further stock decline | Reinforcing | Stock assessment models, recruitment time series | High | Major |
| L2 | Management response cycle | Stock decline -> reduced TAC -> reduced fishing mortality -> stock recovery | Balancing | SESSF Harvest Strategy implementation | Medium | Major |
| L3 | Economic viability loop | Declining catches -> reduced profitability -> fleet consolidation -> increased efficiency -> higher catch rates | Reinforcing | ABARES economic surveys, fleet dynamics data | Medium | Major |
| L4 | Climate-distribution-effort cycle | Ocean warming -> species distribution shifts -> altered fishing efficiency -> changed fishing patterns | Reinforcing | Climate impact research, effort distribution data | Medium | Major |
| L5 | Price-effort feedback | Increased landings -> market saturation -> price decline -> reduced fishing effort | Balancing | Sydney Fish Market data, price-quantity analyses | Medium | Medium |
| L6 | Ecosystem reorganization loop | Fishing pressure -> altered trophic structure -> changed predation/competition -> impeded stock recovery | Reinforcing | Non-recovering stock patterns, ecosystem models | Medium | Major |
| L7 | Technical efficiency cycle | Declining catches -> technological innovation -> increased catchability -> further stock pressure | Reinforcing | CPUE standardization, fishing power studies | Medium | Medium |
| L8 | Market substitution feedback | Reduced domestic supply -> increased prices -> increased imports -> price competition -> economic pressure | Balancing | Market integration studies, price correlation analysis | Medium | Major |
| L9 | Quota value dynamics | Stock status improvement -> increased confidence -> higher quota value -> increased fishing rights investment | Reinforcing | Quota trading data, industry investment patterns | Medium | Medium |
| L10 | Compliance feedback | Increased enforcement -> improved compliance -> reduced illegal take -> stock improvement | Balancing | Enforcement statistics, compliance monitoring | Low | Medium |
