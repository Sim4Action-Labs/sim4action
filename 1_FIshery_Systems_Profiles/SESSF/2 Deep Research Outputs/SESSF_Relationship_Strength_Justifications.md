# SESSF Relationship Strength Justifications

## Strong Relationships

### Core Biological Relationships
**R1: Stock Biomass → Recruitment Rate**
**Strength**: Strong
**Justification**: Well-documented stock-recruitment relationships with consistent data from stock assessments and long-term monitoring. Phase 2 shows robust measurement systems through logbooks, catch disposal records, and vessel monitoring systems. Phase 3 confirms strong feedback mechanisms between stock size and recruitment, with high confidence evidence from stock assessment models and recruitment time series.

**R2: Recruitment Rate → Stock Biomass**
**Strength**: Strong
**Justification**: Direct positive relationship supported by biological principles and stock assessment data. Phase 2 demonstrates clear measurement systems for recruitment monitoring, while Phase 3 shows strong evidence of stock-recruitment relationships in the system dynamics analysis. The relationship is well-quantified through age-structured stock assessments and long-term monitoring programs.

**R3: Mortality Rate → Stock Biomass**
**Strength**: Strong
**Justification**: Clear negative relationship with robust data from catch records and stock assessments. Phase 2 shows comprehensive mortality data collection through logbooks and observer programs. Phase 3 documents strong evidence of mortality impacts on stock biomass, with high confidence in the relationship's direction and magnitude.

**R6: Fishing Mortality → Stock Biomass**
**Strength**: Strong
**Justification**: Well-established relationship with extensive catch and effort data. Phase 2 shows sophisticated monitoring systems including electronic monitoring and observer coverage. **R7: Growth Rate → Stock Biomass**
**Strength**: Strong
